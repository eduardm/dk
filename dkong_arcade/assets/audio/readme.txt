In a production game, this directory would contain the following audio files:

- jump.mp3 - Sound played when the player jumps
- barrel_hit.mp3 - Sound played when a barrel hits the player
- victory.mp3 - Sound played when the player reaches the princess
- gameover.mp3 - Sound played when the player loses all lives
- barrel_roll.mp3 - Sound played as barrels roll down platforms

These sounds would be properly licensed or created specifically for the game.
